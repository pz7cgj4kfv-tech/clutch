By signing, You agree:

**1. Confidential Information.** Everything You learn about Clutch — the concept, the name, the designs, the code, the features, the business model, the data, the users, the finances, the strategy, and any of our discussions — is confidential and the exclusive property of Clutch.

**2. You keep it secret.** You will not share, publish, show, or describe it to anyone, and will not use it for any purpose other than helping Clutch. This obligation lasts during our collaboration and for **five (5) years** afterwards.

**3. You do not copy it.** You will not build, help build, advise, or invest in any product or service that copies or competes with Clutch using what You learned, for **two (2) years** after our collaboration ends.

**4. Your contributions belong to Clutch.** Any idea, feedback, design, code, or improvement You provide becomes the exclusive property of Clutch, with no claim, right, or compensation owed to You — unless agreed separately and in writing (see "Our promise to You").

**5. No poaching, no circumvention.** You will not, for **two (2) years**, solicit Clutch's team, partners, or users away from Clutch, nor bypass Clutch to deal with them directly using what You learned here.

**6. Return.** On Clutch's request, You will promptly delete or return all Clutch materials in Your possession.

**7. Breach.** You acknowledge that a breach would cause Clutch serious and irreparable harm. Clutch may seek an immediate injunction and damages.

**8. Governing law.** This agreement is governed by **Swiss law**. Any dispute is submitted to the **courts of Lausanne (canton of Vaud), Switzerland**.
